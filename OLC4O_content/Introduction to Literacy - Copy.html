Introduction to Literacy

  

Literacy is most commonly defined as the ability to read and write.

It means that you’re able to communicate clearly and effectively.

 

A few examples of literacy include:

  • having the ability to translate speech into written words.
  • understanding the concepts of mathematics.
  • explaining what you’re reading.
