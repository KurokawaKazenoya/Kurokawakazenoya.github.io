Opinion Essay - Body Paragraph

 

Each of these paragraphs should be devoted to one of your points of proof. Write the paragraphs in the following format:

Introductory Sentence

Supporting Fact

Explanation

Concluding sentence

Explain your point of proof

Add at least 2 pieces of support.


Can be a fact, example, situation, etc...

Explain how support proves your point.

Wrap up your argument.

Example:

Cell-phones are very important to have because they help teenagers to stay safe. If a teenager has a cell-phone, they can reach their parents at any time, and their parents can reach them. If teenagers are in a dangerous situation, such as having their car break down, they can quickly call their parents and help if they have a cell-phone. If they did not have a cell-phone, they would have to find another way to reach their parents, which may be difficult and dangerous. If they are in a more difficult situation, for example, if their friend is injured, they can call 911. Without a cell-phone, they would need to possibly leave their injured friend to find help, resulting in the friend’s injury worsening. For these reasons, it was evident that a cell-phone is a necessity for a teenager.
