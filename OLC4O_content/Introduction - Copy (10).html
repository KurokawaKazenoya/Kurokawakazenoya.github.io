Writing Strategies: Body Paragraph

Lesson Description

In this lesson, you will understand the application of opinion pieces in everyday life, relate the writing process and other strategies to write a series of paragraphs for an opinion piece, learn how to write the introductory paragraph, body paragraph, and how to conclude an opinion essay. You will be given tips to improve your opinion essay writing skills. Then, you will apply your learning from the past three lessons in your Classwork.

Overall Expectations

B1 Demonstrate the ability to use the writing process by generating and organizing ideas and producing first drafts, revised drafts, and final polished pieces to complete a variety of writing tasks.

B2 Use knowledge of writing forms and of the connections between form, audience, and purpose, to write summaries, information paragraphs, opinion pieces.

Specific Expectations

B1.2 Use pre writing strategies to generate ideas for writing.

B1.5 Use appropriate strategies to organize ideas and information for writing.

B1.6 Create a first draft that includes the main and supporting ideas in the required form.

B1.7 Revise drafts to ensure that ideas are presented in a logical order, to discard irrelevant ideas and information, to add details Where information is insufficient, and to ensure a tone and level of language appropriate to the audience and purpose, using appropriate strategies.

B1.9 Use appropriate strategies to edit written work.

B2.1 Explain the purpose and uses of information paragraphs.

B2.4 Construct clear, complete information paragraphs, some with graphic elements, for a variety of purposes, using correct Paragraph structure.

Success Criteria

You are successful in this lesson if you can:

  • Understand the purpose and use of opinion pieces.
  • Remember the writing process.
  • Organize collected information and form an opinion.
  • Write a series of paragraphs based on your opinion.
  • Use transition words to link your series of paragraphs together.

Learning Goals

  • Students will learn the purpose and use of opinion pieces.
  • Students will review the writing process to use in writing opinion pieces.
  • Students will learn how to form an opinion based on collected information.
  • Students will learn the steps on writing a series of paragraphs based on their opinion.
  • Students will learn about transition words and their use in writing a series of paragraphs for their opinion piece.
  • Students will practice writing opinion pieces.
