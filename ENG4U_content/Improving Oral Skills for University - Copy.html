Improving Oral Skills for University

To effectively deliver a speech, you can keep these tips in mind.

Open All Panels

Prepare your Speech

  • Clearly, John Waters prepared for this speech.
  • Among all the strategies we could give, it’s always preparation that is key.
  • If you prepare enough, you can successfully deliver anything.

Relate to the audience

  • In John Waters speech, you may notice that he included words and phrases the audience is familiar with like outsider, insider, sooo 2014, and trigger warning.
  • By doing this, the students are engaged with his message.

Change tone or pronunciation with some words.

  • You can notice a change in John Waters’ tone in different parts of his speech.
  • By varying your tone, you can hold your audience’s attention.

Avoid reading everything

  • John Waters wrote his own speech, but we can see in the video that did not prevent him from reading some parts of his speech.
  • It’s okay to glance at copies, but you have to maintain eye contact to engage with your audience.

Use hand gestures considerably

  • John Waters used hand gestures in his delivery of the commencement speech.
  • Notice that he doesn’t use it all the time. He times it in specific points that need emphasis.
