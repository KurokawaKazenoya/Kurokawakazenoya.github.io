Plot

A plot is the series of main events that happen in a story. The five elements of a plot are exposition, rising action, climax, falling action, and resolution. 

Elements of a Plot

Open All Panels

Exposition

Introduces setting, characters, and the conflict serves to entice the reader to keep reading. 

Rising Action

The main part of a story, when the main character tries to solve his or her problem.

Climax

The highest point of interest in the story when a character solves his/her struggles.

Falling Action

The part of the story that leads to the ending or resolution.

Resolution

Refers to the final details of the story. Not all stories have a formal resolution, sometimes stories have a surprise or twist ending.
