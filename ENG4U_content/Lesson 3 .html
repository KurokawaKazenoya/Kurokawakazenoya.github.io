Lesson 3

Students embark on a literary journey exploring the captivating realms of dystopian and utopian literature, investigating their profound relevance to the impact of technology. The class commences with an introduction to these genres, illuminating their societal implications. Through immersive reading and in-depth analysis of selected dystopian and utopian novels, students delve into intricate narratives that dissect the consequences of technological advancements, fostering critical thinking and literary interpretation skills. Vibrant class discussions ensue, allowing students to articulate their insights into the themes and messages conveyed in the literature, encouraging collaborative exploration of complex ideas. The lesson culminates in individual or group presentations, wherein students summarize the main ideas and lessons gleaned from the novels, honing their communication skills and consolidating their understanding of the profound interplay between technology and society as depicted in literature.

What's the Big Idea?

Students will analyze dystopian and utopian literature related to technology.
