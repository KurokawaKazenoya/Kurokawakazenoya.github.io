Lesson 4

Through critical literary analysis, students dissect literary works, delving deep into the ethical questions raised within these narratives. Discussions on the transformative role of literature in raising ecological awareness enhance their empathy and reflective thinking. Simultaneously, linguistic skills are honed as students master the application of conditional perfect tenses, allowing them to express hypothetical situations with grammatical accuracy. Their writing acumen further advances as they ensure paragraph unity, constructing focused and cohesive paragraphs that enhance the clarity and purpose of their writing. Through this holistic approach, students emerge with a heightened understanding of environmental ethics, refined language proficiency, and advanced analytical and writing abilities, empowering them to engage thoughtfully with complex ethical and literary concepts.

What's the Big Idea?

Students will engage in discussions on environmental ethics and eco-criticism.

Success Criteria

  • Examine different ethical frameworks concerning the environment
