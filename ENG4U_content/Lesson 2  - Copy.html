Lesson 2

Students delve into the cutting-edge realms of artificial intelligence and virtual reality, understanding the core concepts and exploring their multifaceted applications. Through vibrant class discussions, students analyze the profound implications of these technologies across diverse fields, including healthcare, entertainment, and education, developing critical insights into the transformative potential of AI and virtual reality.

The lesson deepens with the analysis of relevant articles and video clips, where students discern key advancements, honing their research and evaluative skills. Concurrently, students refine their language proficiency by mastering relative clauses, incorporating relative pronouns seamlessly into complex sentences. Additionally, the lesson hones their writing abilities, emphasizing the art of concise and impactful expression.

By the end, students emerge with a comprehensive understanding of these cutting-edge technologies, fortified analytical skills, and refined language proficiency, empowering them for thoughtful engagement with the evolving world of artificial intelligence and virtual reality.

    

What's the Big Idea?

 Students will explore literary themes related to artificial intelligence and virtual reality.

Success Criteria

  • Examine the implications of artificial intelligence and virtual reality in diverse fields
  • Analyze relevant articles and video clips showcasing advancements in artificial intelligence
  • Construct grammatically correct and coherent sentences that incorporate relative clauses
