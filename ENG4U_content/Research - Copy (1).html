Research

Primarily, when looking for a topic to research, you should stick to what you passionately know. Once you identified that, it’s easy to keep working on your research. Most people's tendency is that they choose topics that they don’t really care about, and in the end, they abandon their research.

In choosing your topic for research, make sure that what you are working on has relevance. The goal is always, always, always, to improve life--make things easier, fair and more ideal for everyone. We aim to understand and not just to prove something wrong.

Know more about your topic! Read background information and the current status of the topic at hand. It helps you figure out the appropriate approach for your research.
