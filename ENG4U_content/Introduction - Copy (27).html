Types of Sentences

Lesson Description

In this lesson, you will learn about types of sentences.

Overall Expectations

  • B2 Understanding Form and Style: recognize a variety of text forms, text features, and stylistic elements and demonstrate understanding of how they help communicate meaning;
  • C2 Using Knowledge of Form and Style: Draft and revise their writing, using a variety of literary, informational, and graphic forms and stylistic elements appropriate for the purpose and audience;
  • C3 Applying Knowledge of Conventions: Use editing, proofreading, and publishing skills and strategies, and knowledge of language conventions, to correct errors, refine expression, and present their work effectively.

Specific Expectations

  • B 2.1 identify a variety of characteristics of literary, informational, and graphic text forms and explain how they help communicate meaning.
  • B 2.2 identify a variety of text features and explain how they help communicate meaning.
  • C 2.4 Write complete sentences that communicate their meaning clearly and accurately, varying sentence type, structure, and length for different purposes and making logical transitions between ideas.
  • C 2.7 Produce revised drafts of both simple and complex texts written to meet criteria identified by the teacher, based on the curriculum expectations.
  • C 3.4 Use grammar conventions correctly to communicate their intended meaning clearly.

Learning Goals

  • Students will learn about conjunctions.
  • Students will use grammar conventions on conjunctions correctly in the quiz to communicate their intended meaning clearly.

Success Criteria

You are successful in this lesson if you can:

  • Define the types of sentences.
  • Identify types of sentences by function and structure.
  • Write different types of sentences.
  • Use grammar conventions correctly in the quiz to communicate their intended meaning clearly.
