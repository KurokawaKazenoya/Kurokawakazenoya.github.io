Review and Organize the Acts of Hamlet

  • Using the provided template, fill in the tables with information pertaining to each of the Acts of Hamlet:
    • Key Characters
    • Settings
    • Identify at Least 5 Main Events
    • Explain 2 Relationships That Are Affected
    • Explain How 1 Particular Theme is Illustrated
  • For the final product, you may be as creative as possible, so long as you show your understanding clearly.
  • The final product can be in this table, a PowerPoint
  • Presentation, or another method of your choosing.

Choose a Soliloquy, Analyze & Write a Response

  • Using the types of dramatic significance handout, your task is to first choose a passage or soliloquy that Shakespeare uses in Hamlet, and then delve deep into the purpose behind it.
  • Remember that some passages will be used for more than one type of dramatic significance (and those that have multiple are usually the best to analyze)! 
  • Your assignment should be at minimum one page, double-spaced, 12 pt font in size.
  • Make reference to the passage or soliloquy (either using the modern No Fear Shakespeare text that we read from or the original Shakespearean text)
  • Explain your connections and ideas as clearly, maturely, and as analytically as you can.
