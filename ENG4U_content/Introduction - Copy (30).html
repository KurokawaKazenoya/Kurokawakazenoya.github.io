Reflection

Lesson Description

In this lesson, you will reflect on your learning in this unit by answering questions about your strengths and weaknesses during this unit, and ways to improve.

Overall Expectations

A3. Reflecting on Skills and Strategies: reflect on and identify their strengths as listeners and speakers, areas for improvement, and the strategies they found most helpful in oral communication situations.

B4. Reflecting on Skills and Strategies: reflect on and identify their strengths as readers, areas for improvement, and the strategies they found most helpful before, during, and after reading.

C4. Reflecting on Skills and Strategies: reflect on and identify their strengths as writers, areas for improvement, and the strategies they found most helpful at different stages in the writing process.

D4. Reflecting on Skills and Strategies: reflect on and identify their strengths as media interpreters and creators, areas for improvement, and the strategies they found most helpful in understanding and creating media texts.

Specific Expectations

A3.1 Demonstrate insight into their strengths and weaknesses as listeners and speakers, and practise the strategies they found most helpful before, during, and after listening and speaking to improve their oral communication skills.

B4.1 demonstrate insight into their strengths and weaknesses as readers and practice the strategies they found most helpful when reading particularly challenging texts to enhance their reading skills.

C4.1 demonstrate insight into their strengths and weaknesses as writers, and practise the strategies they found most helpful when writing particularly complex texts to improve their writing skills.

D4.1 Demonstrate insight into their strengths and weaknesses as media interpreters and producers and practice the strategies they found most helpful when interpreting and creating particularly complex media texts to improve their skills.

Learning Goals

  • Students will reflect on their classwork assignments throughout the unit.
  • Students will identify their strengths and areas for improvement.
  • Students will think of ways to improve their performance.

Success Criteria

You will know you are successful in this lesson if you:

  • I can identify my strengths and areas for improvement in the assigned tasks.
  • I can think of ways to improve to perform better.
  • I can reflect on my classwork assignments throughout the unit.
  • I can identify my strengths and areas for improvement.
  • I can think of ways to improve their performance.
