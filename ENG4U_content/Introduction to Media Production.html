Media: The Facts
  • Media, no matter the form, is not real life. 
  • It does not present reality. It represents it!
  • Media constructs people, places, events, issues, and more. 
  • This is not a bad thing, but it’s best to be aware of this because people consume media every day.

Importance of the Media Production Process

  • Saves the production time, money, and other resources
  • Brings order to a production
  • Refines the media product’s content, duration, and presentation

Why Do We Need to Study Media Production?

  • Being aware of the media production process reminds you that Media Products are not real life.
  • The media affects our experiences, culture, gender, and preconceived expectations. 
  • It is best to know what it is made of and how it is made.
