Characters

Characters are the individuals that are depicted in the story. Characters can be classified as static, dynamic, flat, and round.

Types of Characters:

Open All Panels

Static -

Characters who are barely revealed or do not change throughout the story

Dynamic - 

Characters who change or transform through the story. They are described at great length.

Flat -

A minor character who does not undergo substantial change or growth in the course of the story. 

Round - 

A major character in a work of fiction who encounters a conflict that helps him/her change.

Characterization

Characterization refers to how characters are developed throughout the story using their speech, thoughts, actions, looks, and effects on others.
