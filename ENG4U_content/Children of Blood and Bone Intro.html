Children of Blood and Bone Intro

Orïsha is inspired by locations, culture, and religion in South Africa.

 

The author also used Yoruba, a language used in Nigeria, to write the characters' spells in her book.

 

 

The Clans of Orïsha

Each of the ten clans of&nbsp;Orïsha and their associated Maji can harness an element of the natural world, with their magical powers based on this element.

MAJI

Zél is a Reaper, Majis, who can manipulate life and death.

Along the way, she teams up with other Majis, who can tap into dreams, command water, fire, earth and other elements.
