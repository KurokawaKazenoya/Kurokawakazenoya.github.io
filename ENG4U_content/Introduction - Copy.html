Improving Oral Skills for University

Lesson Description

In this lesson, you will reflect on your oral communication skills and identify effective oral communication techniques. They will apply effective techniques in oral communication in creating a video about a topic of your choosing. They will create a video expressing points about your chosen topic as part of the lesson.

Overall Expectations

A1: Listening to Understand: listen in order to understand and respond appropriately in a variety of situations for a variety of purposes.
A2: Speaking to Communicate: use speaking skills and strategies appropriately to communicate with different audiences for a variety of purposes.
C2: Using Knowledge of Form and Style: draft and revise their writing, using a variety of literary, informational, and graphic forms and stylistic elements appropriate for the purpose and audience.
C3: Applying Knowledge of Conventions: use editing, proofreading, and publishing skills and strategies, and knowledge of language conventions, to correct errors, refine expression, and present their work effectively.
D3: Creating Media Texts: create a variety of media texts for different purposes and audiences, using appropriate forms, conventions, and techniques.

Specific Expectations

A1.9 evaluate the effectiveness of a wide variety of presentation strategies used in oral texts, including complex and challenging texts, and suggest other strategies that could be used effectively.
A2.1 communicate orally for a wide range of purposes, using language effective for the intended audience.
A2.3 communicate in a clear, coherent manner, using a structure and style effective for the purpose, subject matter, and intended audience.
A2.5 identify a variety of vocal strategies, including tone, pace, pitch, and volume, and use them effectively and with sensitivity to audience needs and cultural differences.
A2.6 identify a variety of non-verbal cues, including facial expressions, gestures, and eye contact, and use them effectively to help convey their meaning and with sensitivity to audience needs and cultural differences.
C2.1 Write for different purposes and audiences using a variety of literary, informational, and graphic forms.
C3.4 Use grammar conventions correctly and appropriately to communicate their intended meaning clearly and effectively.
D3.4 produce media texts, including complex texts, for a variety of purposes and audiences, using the most appropriate forms, conventions, and techniques.

Learning Goals

  • Students will reflect on their oral communication skills.
  • Students will identify effective techniques in oral communication in sample videos.
  • Students will apply effective techniques in oral communication in sample videos.
  • Students will create a video using appropriate forms, conventions, and techniques.

Success Criteria

You will know you are successful in this lesson if you:

  • I can reflect on my oral communication skills (strengths, weaknesses).
  • I can identify effective techniques of oral communication from watching sample videos.
  • I can apply these effective techniques of oral communication.
  • I can create a video using appropriate forms, conventions, and techniques.
