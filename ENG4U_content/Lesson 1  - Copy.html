Lesson 1

Beginning with a foundational understanding, students delve into the intricacies of environmental issues, analyzing the profound impact of human activities on the planet. Engaging critically, they examine diverse perspectives on climate change, cultivating a nuanced comprehension of the topic. Simultaneously, students refine their writing abilities, mastering complex sentence structures by integrating subordinate and relative clauses. Additionally, they hone the art of crafting compelling introductions and conclusions, capturing readers' attention and summarizing key points with finesse. Through this multifaceted approach, students not only deepen their environmental awareness but also sharpen their writing skills, equipping themselves to articulate complex concepts persuasively and effectively.

What's the Big Idea?

Students will understand the concept of ecological challenges and climate change.

Success Criteria

  • Construct complex sentences, incorporating subordinate clauses, relative clauses, and coordinating conjunctions
  • Analyze the impact of human activities on the environment
  • Craft impactful introductions for essays, capturing readers' attention with engaging hooks
  • Construct compelling conclusions that effectively summarize key points
