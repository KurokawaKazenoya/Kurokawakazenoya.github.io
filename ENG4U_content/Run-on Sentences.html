Run-on Sentences

  • I am big it’s the pictures that got small.
  • Life is a banquet, most poor people are starving to death.

If you didn’t spot the errors in the sentences above, then you are not aware of a common error: run-on sentences.

  • This is formed when two independent clauses are joined incorrectly.
  • There are two types of run-on sentences:

  1. Fused sentences

  • I am big it’s the pictures that got small.
  • Comma Splice
  • Life is a banquet, most poor people are starving to death.

Fused sentences

  • These are clauses joined directly: no punctuation marks, no conjunctions.Example: 
  • You are kind you are smart you are important.

Comma splice

  • These clauses are joined only by using a comma.Example: 
  • A million dollars isn’t cool, you know what’s cool?

There are ways to correct these errors. Let’s go through them using the sample sentence: I wanted you badly, I thought you wanted me too.

Use a comma and a coordinating conjunction (and, but, yet, so, or, nor, for).

  • It was really catchy, so this line made it into each Godfather film in some way or another..

Use a semicolon.

  • You can use a semicolon alone or with a transition word (e.g., however, at any rate, in contrast, as a result, etc.)

  • What we’ve got here is failure to communicate; I feel like you don’t listen to any word I say.

For longer run-on sentences like this one, 

 

“Robert DeNiro sometimes improvised his lines in his movie Taxi Driver, the script only said “Travis speaks to himself in the mirror”, DeNiro changed it up and was successful.”

you can do the following strategies.

Separate the independent clauses into sentences. Restructure the sentence by subordinating one of the clauses.

Robert DeNiro sometimes improvised his lines. In his movie Taxi Driver, the script only said “Travis speaks to himself in the mirror” but DeNiro changed it up and was successful.

Although the script only said “Travis speaks to himself in the mirror”, Robert DeNiro improvised his lines and was successful in his movie Taxi Driver.
