Inspiration Behind Children of Blood and Bone

Another force that drove Adeyemi to write her book filled with "unmistakably black characters" was the racist backlash against The Hunger Games' film adaptation.

Some viewers denounced "black" actors getting prominent roles in the movie.

 

About the author

Tomi Adeyemi is a 27-year-old Nigerian-American writer and creative writing coach.

She has an honours degree in literature and received a fellowship that allowed her to study West African culture in Brazil.

The idea of her book came when she was visiting a gift shop in Salvador, Brazil.

 

Movie Adaptation

The movie rights for Children of Blood and Bone has been reportedly picked up by Disney's Lucasfilm, home to the legendary Star Wars and Indiana Jones franchises.
