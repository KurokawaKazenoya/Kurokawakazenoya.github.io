Conflict

The conflict is the struggle between two people or things in a story. It is the central source of tension in the story. 

Types of Conflict

There are two main types of conflict present in most stories: external and internal. 

Internal conflict is the character’s internal struggle with himself. This is also referred to as “man vs. self” conflict. 

External conflict sets the character against external forces beyond his/her control such as another character, nature, society, technology, or supernatural.
