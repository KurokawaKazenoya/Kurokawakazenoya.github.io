Introductory Video, Goal-Setting and University and Reading and Writing Exercises

Lesson Description

In this lesson, students will first discuss their ideas and plans for postsecondary education. They will read articles discussing the purposes and goals of attending a University. After this, they will look at the importance of goal-setting, and understand what SMART goals look like. Students will then create a video introducing themselves using appropriate speaking skills and strategies. Also in this video they will reflect on post-secondary plans and learn about the purpose and importance of universities. They will think about what degree they plan to take and where they plan on studying.

Overall Expectations

B1: Reading for Meaning: read and demonstrate an understanding of a variety of literary, informational, and graphic texts, using a range of strategies to construct meaning.

C2: Using Knowledge of Form and Style: draft and revise their writing, using a variety of literary, informational, and graphic forms and stylistic elements appropriate for the purpose and audience;

C3: Applying Knowledge of Conventions: use editing, proofreading, and publishing skills and strategies, and knowledge of language conventions, to correct errors, refine expression, and present their work effectively; 

Specific Expectations

B1.1 Read a variety of student- and teacher-selected texts from diverse cultures and historical periods, identifying specific purposes for reading

B1.4 Make and explain inferences of increasing subtlety and insight about texts, including complex and challenging texts, supporting their explanations with well-chosen stated and implied ideas from the texts.

B1.5 Extend understanding of texts, including complex and challenging texts, by making rich and increasingly insightful connections between the ideas in them and personal knowledge, experience, and insights; other texts; and the world around them.

C2.1 Write for different purposes and audiences using a variety of literary, informational, and graphic forms

C2.4 Write complete sentences that communicate their meaning clearly and effectively, skilfully varying sentence type, structure, and length to suit different purposes and making smooth and logical transitions between ideas

C3.4 Use grammar conventions correctly and appropriately to communicate their intended meaning clearly and effectively 

Learning Goals

  • Students will reflect on and identify strengths as listeners and speakers, readers, writers, media interpreters and creators, areas for improvement, and the most helpful strategies in English.
  •  Students will use speaking skills and strategies appropriately to communicate through a video.
  •  Students will create a video for an introduction, using appropriate forms, conventions, and techniques.
  • Students will reflect on their post-secondary plans.
  • Students will understand the purpose of a university 

Success Criteria

  • I can reflect on and identify my strengths as a listener and speaker, reader, writer, media interpreter and creator, my areas for improvement, and the most helpful strategies in English.
  • I can use speaking skills and strategies appropriately to communicate through a video.
  • I can create a video for an introduction, using appropriate forms, conventions, and techniques.
  • I can reflect on my post-secondary plans.
  • I can understand the purpose of a university.
