Unit 1 - Oral Communication Skills Summative Task

Lesson Description

In this lesson, you will complete an assessment of your learning for this unit that is provided by your teacher. To complete this lesson, you should:

  1. Notify your teacher that you are ready to complete your unit assessment of learning. You will first go through the Assignment Information, and will then proceed through the three steps of the Assessment.
  2. Complete the assessment of learning given to you by your teacher in the agreed upon timeframe.
  3. Submit your completed work to your teacher by putting a copy of your work in your shared course folder.

Overall Expectations

A2. Speaking to Communicate: use speaking skills and strategies appropriately to communicate with different audiences for a variety of purposes.

C1 Developing and Organizing Content: generate, gather, and organize ideas and information to write for an intended purpose and audience.

C2 Using Knowledge of Form and Style: draft and revise their writing, using a variety of literary, informational, and graphic forms and stylistic elements appropriate for the purpose and audience.

D3 Creating Media Texts: create a variety of media texts for different purposes and audiences, using appropriate forms, conventions, and techniques.

Specific Expectations

A2.1 communicates orally for a wide range of purposes, using language effective for the intended audience.

A2.3 communicates in a clear, coherent manner, using a structure and style effective for the purpose, subject matter, and intended audience.

A2.4 uses the most appropriate words, phrases, terminology, and a variety of stylistic devices to communicate their meaning in a compelling way and to engage their intended audience.

A2.5 identify a variety of vocal strategies, including tone, pace, pitch, and volume, and use them effectively and with sensitivity to audience needs and cultural differences.

A2.6 identify a variety of non-verbal cues, including facial expressions, gestures, and eye contact, and use them effectively to help convey their meaning and with sensitivity to audience needs and cultural differences.

C1.1 identify the topic, purpose, and audience for a variety of writing tasks.

C1.2 generates, expands, explores, and focuses ideas for potential writing tasks, using a variety of strategies and print, electronic, and other resources, as appropriate.

C1.3 locate and select information to fully and effectively support ideas for writing, using a variety of strategies and print, electronic, and other resources, as appropriate.

C2.2 establishes a distinctive and original voice in their writing, modifying language and tone skillfully and effectively to suit the form, audience, and purpose of writing.

C2.6 revise drafts to improve the content, organization, clarity, and style of their written work.

D3.4 produces media texts, including complex texts, for a variety of purposes and audiences, using the most appropriate forms, conventions, and techniques.

Learning Goals

  • Students will write a speech for an oral presentation
  • Students will create an oral presentation based on their topic and speech.
  • Students will deliver their oral presentations using the skills discussed in this unit.

Success Criteria

You will know you are successful in this lesson if you:

  • I can write a speech for an oral presentation.
  • I can create an oral presentation based on my topic and speech.
  • I can deliver my oral presentation using the skills discussed in this unit.
