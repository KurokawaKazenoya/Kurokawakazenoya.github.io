Lesson

The class commences with an exploration of privacy, data security, and surveillance in the digital landscape, fostering awareness of the ethical implications. Engaging with real-life case studies, students delve into discussions, honing their critical thinking skills by navigating complex ethical dilemmas related to technology. Simultaneously, linguistic mastery advances as students grasp the nuances of advanced passive voice constructions, emphasizing actions over doers for impactful communication. Further, critical analysis skills are honed within paragraphs, where students seamlessly integrate opinions with textual evidence, constructing compelling arguments and interpretations. This multifaceted approach equips students with ethical awareness, advanced language proficiency, and analytical acumen, empowering them to navigate the complex intersection of technology and ethics with depth and insight.

What's the Big Idea?

Students will examine the ethical considerations and privacy issues related to technology.

Success Criteria

  • Integrate critical analysis within paragraphs, skillfully combining opinions with textual evidence to construct coherent arguments and interpretations
  • Analyze real-life case studies, engaging in discussions about ethical dilemmas related to technology
  • Master advanced passive voice constructions
