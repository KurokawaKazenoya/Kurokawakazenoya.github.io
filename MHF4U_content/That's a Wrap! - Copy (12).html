That's a Wrap!

Learning Goals

Students will review the concept of slope.

Students can calculate and interpret the average rate of change on an interval of the independent variable.

Students can find the average rate of change of a function between two points algebraically and graphically.

Students know what secant lines are and can explain how they can be used to find average rates of changes in functions.

Students can estimate and interpret the rate of change at a particular value of the independent variable.

Students can give real word examples of instantaneous rate of change.

Students can estimate instantaneous rates of change using slopes of lines.

Students know what tangent lines are and can use them to estimate the instantaneous rate of change at a point.

Students can represent verbal descriptions of rates of change using graphs.

Students can use rates of change to solve problems that involve functions.
