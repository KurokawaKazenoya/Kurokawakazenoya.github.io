Graphs of Reciprocal Functions

  1. Any point         becomes
  2. Reciprocals of linear or quadratic will have a horizontal asymptote at y = 0
  3. Zeros become vertical asymptotes.
  4. The original and reciprocal will be positive and negative on the same intervals.
  5. Intervals of increase/decrease are reversed on reciprocal.
  6. Any local max/min points become local min/max points (they are reversed)
  7. Any point on the original function with a y­-value of 1 or ­1 will intersect the reciprocal at that point.
