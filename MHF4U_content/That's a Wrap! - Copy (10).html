Learning Goals

Students will explore the characteristics of new functions created by combining functions.

Students can represent the sums and differences of two functions graphically and algebraically and determine their properties.

Students can represent the product of two functions graphically and algebraically, and determine the characteristics of the product.

Students can represent the quotient of two functions graphically and algebraically, and determine the characteristics of the quotient.

Students can determine the composition of two functions numerically, graphically, and algebraically.

Students can solve equations and inequalities that involve combinations of functions using a variety of techniques.

Success Criteria

You are successful in this lesson if you….

 I can represent the sums and differences of two functions graphically and algebraically and determine their properties.
