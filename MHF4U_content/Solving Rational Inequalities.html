Solving Rational Inequalities

  • DO NOT cross multiply. Rearrange all terms to one side, the other side zero. 
  • Write expression with single, common denominator, removing any common factors (holes).
  • Determine any vertical asymptotes from the simplified denominator.
  • Determine any zeroes from the simplified numerator.
  • Use an interval table (sign chart) to check for positive and negative intervals.
  • Write your solution, keeping in mind ALL restrictions (holes, VAs, real-world considerations).
